title: Agreements & Customer Support
---

## I. Feature Overview
Used to configure auxiliary content in the App, including the user agreement and online customer support links. With this module, merchants can meet platform compliance requirements, provide necessary support entry points, and enhance user trust and platform professionalism.

## II. Feature Details

**User Agreement**

Merchants must provide a user agreement. The system will automatically display it during user registration and in the About Us section, ensuring users provide informed consent before using the platform services.

**Customer Support Link**

You can configure a link that redirects to an external customer support platform (e.g., Zendesk, Intercom, WhatsApp). Users can quickly access it and start a consultation via entry points such as the App Settings page and Help Center.
