---
title: Configure Custody Wallet
---

To smoothly onboard KeySecure payment card services, please follow the steps below to set up and configure your MPC wallet.

<Info>
The following process is only required for Saas+ merchants.
Saas merchants do not need to configure a custody wallet.
</Info>

## I. Download and Initialize KeySecure App
### Step 1: Download the App
Click the link below to download and install KeySecure App:
[Download](https://www.keysecure.io/download-app/)

### Step 2: Create MPC Workspace & Custody Wallet
Follow the guide below to initialize your wallet and **securely back up your private keys**:
[Wallet Creation Guide](https://docs.keysecure.io/user-guide/mpc-wallet/wallet/create-wallet)

After completion, provide the following information to the KeySecure customer team:

* Merchant Name
* Workspace ID

KeySecure will activate the required blockchain networks and custody infrastructure for you according to your contract once this information is received.

## II. Enable Auto Sweep & Configure Strategy
### Step 2.1: Enable Auto Sweep
Log in to the MPC Web console and enable currency auto sweep:
[Enable Auto Sweep Guide](https://docs.keysecure.io/user-guide/mpc-wallet/consolidate/co-signer/execute)

### Step 2.2: Configure Auto Sweep Rules
Set automatic sweep rules so that user deposits are automatically swept:
[Auto Sweep Rules Configuration](https://docs.keysecure.io/user-guide/mpc-wallet/consolidate/co-signer/rules)

<Note>
If you need to enable TRON energy delegation, confirm the activation process and fees with the team in advance:
[TRON Energy Delegation Guide](https://docs.keysecure.io/help-center/manage-mpc/Tron-delegation/resource-from-custody)
</Note>

## III. Configure API Signing (Cosigner)
To support automated withdrawals and authorized spending, complete the following configuration:

[API Integration Guide](https://docs.keysecure.io/user-guide/mpc-wallet/api-integration/api)

[Cosigner Configuration Guide](https://docs.keysecure.io/user-guide/mpc-wallet/api-integration/co-signer)

## IV. Contact KeySecure to Configure Merchant App Information
Prepare and submit the following information to the KeySecure customer team:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Merchant Info | Name, account, or email (any is acceptable) |
| MPC Wallet Info | Workspace ID, Wallet App ID, Sweep Wallet ID, RSA Public Key |
| Messaging Services | SMS (e.g., Twilio), Email (e.g., SMTP) |

KeySecure will assist you with the configuration and preparation needed for go-live.

<Tip>
For questions, contact your account manager or reach out via:

Official Email: service@keysecure.io
</Tip>
