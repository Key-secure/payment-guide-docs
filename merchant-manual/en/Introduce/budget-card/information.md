An innovative digital currency payment application dedicated to enabling you to easily convert crypto assets into daily spending power. With our virtual payment card, you can conveniently spend your digital assets at online and offline merchants worldwide, just like using a traditional bank card.

## Core Features

PayFun offers the following key functions:

### 1. Real-time Conversion Payment
- **Instantly convert digital currencies into fiat for payments**
- Support instant exchange of multiple mainstream digital currencies
- Transparent exchange rates and clear conversion fees

### 2. Global Payment Network
- **Support merchants worldwide for truly borderless transactions**
- Cover millions of online and offline merchants
- Support multiple payment scenarios and methods

### 3. Security Assurance System
- **Secure and reliable asset management system**
- Multiple encryption technologies to ensure fund security
- Professional risk control system

### 4. Transaction Management
- **Real-time transaction notifications and consumption record inquiry**
- Detailed transaction history records
- Intelligent consumption categorization and analysis

### 5. Multi-currency Support
- **Support multiple mainstream digital currencies**
- Including but not limited to: BTC, ETH, USDT, etc.
- Continuously expanding to support more digital currencies

### 6. User Experience
- **Intuitive interface and easy operation**
- Concise and clear operation process
- Intelligent user guidance
