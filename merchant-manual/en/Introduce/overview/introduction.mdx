A tailored white-label crypto card system for institutional clients, enabling enterprises to rapidly launch branded global payment cards and manage the full lifecycle from card issuance and asset custody to card spending.

## What Are We Building?

We provide a compliant, secure, and fast-to-deploy crypto U-card solution. It helps exchanges, wallets, and fintech companies issue their own branded crypto payment cards worldwide, delivering a safe, compliant, and rich spending experience for end users.

No need to build an in-house R&D team or complex systems. Through the KeySecure platform, you can access card issuance, user management, fund custody, and more in a single place—creating a complete ecosystem where users can easily hold, manage, and spend their crypto assets.

## Our Product Advantages

We simplify complex underlying technologies into 5 core modules, giving you a complete, ready-to-use white-label card ecosystem.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="One-Stop Integration with Deep Brand Customization" icon="handshake">
    - Multiple card BIN options, supporting both virtual and physical cards for rich online and offline spending scenarios  
    - Deep integration of card management, asset management, and marketing management capabilities  
    - Branded app UI and email templates to match your identity  
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Asset Custody with Security and Compliance" icon="shield-check">
    - MPC (Multi-Party Computation) wallets for secure asset protection, with hot/cold wallet separation and independent private key holding  
    - Risk-control strategies for deposits and withdrawals, with automated KYT and KYC checks  
    - Operate without needing your own custody license  
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Global Payment Scenarios" icon="globe">
    - Support for user top-ups in mainstream stablecoins such as USDT and USDC  
    - Real-time FX settlement at the time of spending, covering both online and offline merchants

  <Accordion title="Real-Time Experience with Fund Security" icon="bolt">
