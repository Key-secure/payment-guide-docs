### Prerequisite for Operation
First-time use of PayFun services

### Core Steps
1. Open the App
2. Tap the Register button
3. Fill in the registration information as prompted
4. Complete account creation

### Notes
Please use a valid and authentic email address

---

## Detailed Registration Process

### Step 1: Launch the App
- Download and install the PayFun App on your mobile device
- Tap the App icon to launch PayFun

### Step 2: Access the Registration Page
- Tap the "Register" or "Create Account" button on the welcome page
- If you already have an account, select "Log In" to access the login process

### Step 3: Fill in Registration Information
**Required information usually includes**:
- Mobile phone number or email address
- Set a login password
- Confirm password
- Verification code (obtained via SMS or email)
- Invitation code (if applicable)

### Step 4: Complete Registration
- Agree to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
- Tap the "Complete Registration" or "Create Account" button
- The system will verify the information and create your account

---

## Important Reminders

### Information Accuracy
- **Contact Method**: Ensure your mobile phone number or email can normally receive verification messages
- **Password Security**: It is recommended to use a strong password containing letters, numbers, and special characters
- **Terms Reading**: Please carefully read the User Agreement and Privacy Policy before registration

### Post-Registration Operations
After successful registration, we recommend that you:
1. Complete your personal profile
2. Set a payment password
3. Finish KYC identity verification (subsequent step)
4. Bind a security verification method
