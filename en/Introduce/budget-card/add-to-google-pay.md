- **Prerequisite Operations**:
  - Not installed: Search for 「Google Wallet」 in Google Play Store to download and install
  - First-time launch: Log in to your Google Account → Tap 「Get Started」
- **Action**: Open the installed Google Pay/Google Wallet app

## Global Security Reminders

**Reminders**:
1. Fraud Prevention: Do not enter passwords/verification codes on third-party websites when adding cards; guard against phishing attacks
2. Device Security: Set a lock screen password, fingerprint, or facial recognition to prevent unauthorized use
3. Transaction Monitoring: Check transaction records in real time via the PayFun App to ensure transparent transaction details

## Usage Tips

### Preparation
1. **Account Preparation**: Ensure you are logged in to a valid Google Account
2. **Card Information**: Prepare the complete details of your PayFun virtual card or physical card

### Payment Methods
- **Offline Payments**: Unlock your phone and hold it near the POS terminal's NFC reader
- **Online Payments**: Select Google Pay as the payment method on supported Apps or websites
- **Google Services**: Can be used to pay for Google Play Store, YouTube Premium, and other services

### Frequently Asked Questions
#### Q: Why can't I find the option to add a card?
A: Please ensure your Google Pay app is updated to the latest version and check if your device supports NFC functionality.

#### Q: What should I do if the verification process fails?
A: Check your network connection, ensure the entered verification code is correct, or try switching verification methods (SMS/email).

#### Q: Why can't I use the card after binding?
A: Confirm that your PayFun card is activated in the App and has sufficient balance, and check if your device's NFC function is enabled.
