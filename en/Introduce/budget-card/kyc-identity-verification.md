## Overview
**Description**: KYC Identity Verification Process
**Function Description**: Ensure account security and serve as a necessary prerequisite for activating payment cards (Know Your Customer)

---

## Verification Operation Steps

1. Access the KYC verification page in the App
2. Submit identity information as prompted (ID card/passport, etc.)
3. Wait for system review
4. Complete verification after approval

---

## Detailed Verification Process

### Step 1: Access the KYC Verification Page

#### Access Paths
1. **App Homepage Entry**:
   - Log in to the PayFun App
   - Tap "My" or Personal Center in the bottom navigation bar
   - Locate the "Identity Verification" or "KYC Verification" entry

2. **Guided Entry**:
   - Automatic verification prompt when making the first payment attempt
   - Mandatory step in the card application process
   - Verification option in account security settings

### Step 2: Submit Identity Information

#### Supported Document Types
- **Mainland China Users**:
  - Second-Generation Resident Identity Card (Recommended)
  - Passport
  - Hong Kong/Macao Resident Travel Permit to Mainland China
  - Taiwan Resident Travel Permit to Mainland China

- **International Users**:
  - Passport
  - Driver's License
  - National ID Card

#### Information Submission Requirements

**Basic Information Filling**:
1. **Full Name**:
   - Exactly match the name on the document
   - Fill in pinyin/English name as per the document
   - Distinguish between first name and last name (per local customs)

2. **Document Information**:
   - Select document type
   - Enter document number accurately
   - Verify expiration date

3. **Contact Information**:
   - Residential address (recommended to match billing address)
   - Contact phone number (verified mobile number)

4. **Document Photo Upload Requirements**:
   - Information Consistency: Must fully match the filled document information (name, document number, etc.) to ensure identity verification success;
   - Clarity and Completeness: Photos must be clear, free of glare and obstructions, with complete document edges and legible content;
   - Compliance: Upload original photos taken directly, no copies, screenshots, or modified images allowed.

5. **Selfie Shooting Requirements**:
   - Sufficient Lighting: Choose an environment with adequate natural light or uniform lighting, avoid backlighting and facial shadows;
   - Complete Frame: Face the camera directly with all facial features clearly visible (no hats, sunglasses, masks, or other obstructions), and the head should occupy an appropriate proportion of the frame;
   - Authenticity and Clarity: No beauty filters or retouching allowed, photos must be clear and free of distortion to ensure facial features match the document photo.
