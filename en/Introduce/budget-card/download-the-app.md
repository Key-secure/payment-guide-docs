## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why do I receive a "Device incompatible" prompt during download?
**A:**
- **iOS Users**: Check if your device supports iOS 12.0 or later
- **Android Users**: Check if your Android version is 8.0 or later

### Q: What should I do if the download speed is very slow?
**A:**
- Switch to a Wi-Fi network or use mobile data
- Avoid peak network usage hours
- Clear storage space on your mobile phone
- Restart your router or mobile phone

### Q: How to resolve installation failures?
**A:**
- **Insufficient storage space**: Delete unnecessary files and applications
- **Permission issues**: Check and grant the necessary installation permissions
- **System restrictions**: Review your device's security settings
- **Corrupted files**: Re-download the installation package
